Spooktober!






Dubai International Academy, a joyful and confounding school filled to the brim with wide-eyed, warm-hearted students,
is the ideal place to be three-hundred and fifty-eight days of the year, but one frightful week, the week of Halloween, mischief ensues.
During this week, there is no hiding, no running, and definitely no peace of mind. Chaos erupts left, right, and center as equipment is sabotaged, beloved belongings go missing, and a grim blanket of panic is laid over the school. Every year, a myriad of new problems afflicts the students.


This one abysmal week serves as a creative outlet for a very mysterious and dastardly individual.
